Features

Everything you need to run models in production.

One platform for deployment, scaling, and observability. No glue code, no five-vendor stack, no guessing what your models are doing.

Low-latency inference

Cold starts under 200ms and p95 response times in the low tens of milliseconds, measured at the region closest to each user.

GPU autoscaling

Scale from zero to thousands of GPUs and back in seconds. Pay per request, never for idle accelerators waiting on traffic.

Live tracing

Every request becomes a structured trace: tokens, GPU time, cache hits, and cost, streamed to your dashboard as it happens.

Security and compliance

SOC 2 Type II, private networking, per-region data residency, and signed model artifacts so nothing ships unverified.

Global edge regions

Deploy once and serve from 30+ regions across five continents. Lumina routes each call to the lowest-latency healthy node.

Versioned rollouts

Promote a new model version behind a canary, watch the metrics diverge in real time, and roll back with a single command.

See every request, not just the failures.

Lumina turns inference into first-class telemetry. Open any trace and step through prompt, tokens, GPU time, cache behavior, and cost, all correlated to the exact model version that served it.

Edge network

Inference that lives next to your users.

Models replicate to every region automatically. Lumina health-checks each node, drains the unhealthy ones, and routes traffic to the closest accelerator with capacity, with zero config from you.

  • Automatic multi-region replication
  • Latency-aware request routing
  • Self-healing nodes with live failover
