I want to build a Balancing Birth to Baby website that provides childbirth education and doula services in the Waterloo Region. When someone opens the site, they see a homepage with a hero section saying "Everything is about to change... And, we're here to help!" The main navigation menu at the top has several categories including Classes & Events, Doula, Birth Coaching, Shop, About, Blog, and a shopping cart icon.

If I click on "Classes & Events" in the main menu, I can see a dropdown menu with various options. Clicking "Prenatal Classes" from this dropdown takes me to the Prenatal Classes page. This page shows information about weekend and weeknight prenatal classes, with descriptions of what's covered in the curriculum. The page displays a list of upcoming classes with specific dates and locations, and each class has a "REGISTER" button that allows users to sign up. Near the bottom of the page, there's a "SEE FULL CALENDAR" button. At the bottom of the page, there's a section showing other related classes like Natural Pain Management, Baby + Me Yoga, and other classes, which each have a "SEE DETAILS" button to view more information and register. There's also another "SEE FULL CALENDAR" button at the very bottom to view all classes.

From the "Classes & Events" dropdown menu in the main navigation, I can click on "Class Calendar" which takes me to a calendar page. This page displays all upcoming classes and events in a monthly calendar format. At the top of the calendar, there are filter options including a "Category" dropdown to filter by class type, a search box to find specific classes, and a "Select Month" dropdown with year selector to navigate to different months. The page also has "MONTHLY" and "WEEKLY" view toggle buttons. I can navigate between months using the "DECEMBER" and "FEBRUARY" arrows at the top of the calendar.

If I go back to the homepage and click on "Doula" in the navigation menu, I can select "Birth Doula" from the dropdown. This takes me to the Birth Doula Services page, which explains what doulas do and describes their shared-care team model. The page has a prominent call-to-action button that says "BOOK A FREE CONSULTATION" for scheduling appointments. The page also has multiple sections explaining their services and team approach, and there are additional "BOOK A FREE CONSULTATION" buttons throughout the page for easy access to scheduling.

From the main navigation, clicking "Blog" takes me to the blog page. This page shows recent articles in a list format with featured images, titles, excerpts, author names, and publication dates. On the right sidebar, there are filters including a "Categories" section where I can select topics like Baby, Birth, Postpartum, or Pregnancy to view specific types of articles. The sidebar also shows "Recent Posts" and "Upcoming Classes" sections. Each blog post has a "Read more" link that opens the full article. At the bottom of the page, there's numbered pagination to browse older posts.

Clicking "Shop" in the main navigation takes me to the shop page. This page displays products organized in a grid layout, including gift cards, postpartum healing kits (like Padsicle Kit and Postpartum Healing Bath Kit), equipment rentals like TENS machines and Birth Box Rental, digital guides (like The 4th Trimester Guide And Workbook), and other products. Each product shows an image, title, price, and either an "Add to cart" button or a "Select amount" button (for gift cards). Products are tagged with categories like Gift Cards, Products, or Guides to help users find what they need.

From any page on the site, I can click "About" in the navigation to reach the About Us page. This page introduces the organization's mission and history, explains their doula services and support model, and includes detailed profiles of the team members with their credentials and personal statements. The page contains a "BOOK A FREE CONSULTATION" button to connect with their services, and includes FAQ sections about their doula services.

The homepage itself contains several call-to-action buttons that provide quick navigation: there are "LEARN MORE ABOUT US" buttons that go to the About Us page, a "SEE FULL CALENDAR" button in the "CLASSES" section that goes to the Class Calendar page, a "LEARN MORE" button in the "DOULA SERVICES" section that goes to the Birth Doula Services page, and a "LEARN MORE" button in the "COACHING" section that goes to the Birth Coaching page. The homepage also displays featured blog posts with "READ MORE" links that go to individual blog articles, and shows upcoming classes with buttons that link to class details and registration. At the bottom of the homepage, there are buttons to "LEARN HOW OUR CLASSES & EVENTS" and "LEARN ABOUT OUR DOULA SERVICES" that navigate to the respective service pages.

All pages include the main navigation menu at the top and footer links at the bottom, so I can always navigate back to the homepage or access any major section from anywhere on the site. The footer contains links organized into sections: Services (Doula, Classes), Resources (Blog, Careers), Legal (Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Terms & Conditions), and Social (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter).
